Ammo Crisis

Ammo Crisis is a third person survival shooter prototype, I designed during my first year at university. Using a university provided Unreal Engine 4 framework, I was responsible for making a game design document which:


- Has a multitude of maps that breaks down level flow.

- Diagrams that visualise UI, weapon, and gameplay mechanics.

- Analysis of weapons, enemy and gameplay mechanic behaviour. 

- Collated testing results, which aim to create a balanced gameplay experience with good game feel.


Upon documentation being completed, I used it as a direct reference to fully construct the level within the required Ammo Crisis framework.

Bounty

Bounty is a 2D action-adventure side scroller, I conceptualised and designed during my first year at university. To achieve this, I created a High Concept Game Design Document. Which focuses on high concept analysis for Bounty's fundamental gameplay requirements and features. This included performing:


- Extensive research on similar 2D side scrollers. Resulting in a well-rounded overview of the game.

- Further analysis on how accessibility settings, audio and music can be used to lower the barrier of entry and enhance the player experience.

- Outlined technical software and hardware requirements Bounty will need to meet. So, development of the game remains feasible.
